HOW TO WIN:
1.REACH YOUR HOME BASE BEFORE THE WOLF
CATCHES YOU DURING LUNCHTIME OR

2.CATCH THE WOLF BEFORE LUNCHTIME

RULES:

1.THE NUMBER OF STEPS YOU TAKE MUST BE
THE SAME AS THE TIME(E.G 1 PM->1 STEP)

2.YOU CANNOT TAKE 3 CONSECUTIVE STEPS
OF THE SAME DIRECTION AT ANY TIME

3.YOU MUST RETRACE THE EXACT STEPS YOU
TOOK BACK TO HOME BASE DURING LUNCHTIME
OTHERWISE YOU'LL BE LOST IN THE WOODS
AND DIE

CLICK THE BUTTON TO START PLAYING
